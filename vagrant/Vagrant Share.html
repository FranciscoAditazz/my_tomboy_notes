Vagrant Share

Vagrant Share lets you share your Vagrant environment to anyone around the world. It will give you a URL that will route directly to your Vagrant environment from any device in the world that is connected to the internet. Before being able to share your Vagrant environment, you'll need an account on Vagrant Cloud. Once you have an account, log in using vagrant login: $ vagrant login: Username or Email: mitchellh Password (will be hidden): You're now logged in! Once you're logged in, run vagrant share: $ vagrant share ... ==> default: Your Vagrant Share is running! ==> default: URL: http://frosty-weasel-0857.vagrantshare.com ... Your URL will be different, so don't try the URL above. Instead, copy the URL that vagrant share outputted for you and visit that in a web browser. It should load the index page we setup in the previous pages. Now, modify your "index.html" file and refresh the URL. It will be updated! That URL is routing directly into your Vagrant environment, and works from any device in the world that is connected to the internet. Vagrant Share is much more powerful than simply HTTP sharing. See complete Vagrant Share documentation https://docs.vagrantup.com/v2/share/
