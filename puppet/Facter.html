Facter

Facter is one of the many prepackaged tools that Puppet Enterprise ships with. Facter is Puppet's system inventory tool. Facter reads facts about a node (such as its hostname, IP address, operating system, etc.) and makes them available to Puppet. The Facter tool discovers information about your system and makes it available to Puppet as Puppet Variables. Puppet’s compiler accesses those facts when it’s reading a manifest. You can see all the facts by running `facter -p`.Examples: facter ipaddress facter uptime_hours Reference: http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/precise/man8/facter.8.html
